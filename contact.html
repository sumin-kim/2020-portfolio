Who am I?

More stimulating, more daring. In a media market where content that tries to cross the line is so popular, I am still dreaming of a producer with a good influence. I always think about how to bring out the witty comfort and empathy that entertainment can give, and above all, laughter and fun. Beyond words and text, I think a video is the highest level of 'language' used today. I want to become a producer that communicates with people through video and gives them fun and excitement from empathy.
